### Technology & Architecture

**Core mechanism: Proof-of-Intelligence**

Bittensor differs fundamentally from Bitcoin by using proof-of-intelligence rather than energy-intensive proof-of-work. Nodes must perform AI-related computational tasks to prove their capability, with rewards distributed based on task output quality.

**Subnet structure and validation**

The network operates through specialized subnets that function as incentivized marketplaces for decentralized machine learning. Each subnet operates semi-independently while contributing to the broader network's AI innovation capacity.

**Token emission and mining mechanics**

TAO emissions serve as economic incentives for network participants, directly analogous to Bitcoin's BTC emissions. The halving mechanism ensures token scarcity increases while maintaining predictable supply growth with a four-year halving cycle.
